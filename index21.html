The design of the GLC

Exterior

Exterior equipment of the new GLC.

Everything that makes the "Sensual Purity" design concept and modern luxury so attractive is combined in the GLC. The design of the new series has been modernized accordingly . A sporty SUV design that conveys intelligence and emotion in equal measure and that already makes an impression as a production vehicle with the AVANTGARDE Line exterior.

Interior

Interior equipment of the new GLC.

The flowing shapes and the fine materials raise the modern luxury in the interior of the GLC to a new level. The stylish, sporty cockpit is even more focused on the driver. The revised operating and display concept, which relies fully on digitization, also contributes to this.
