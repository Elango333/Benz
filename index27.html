The design of the Mercedes-AMG

Exterior

The performers in the compact class.

The new Mercedes-AMG A 45 S 4MATIC+ embodies individual sportiness from every perspective. The exterior already conveys top performance when stationary and immediately suggest sheer Driving Performance. The striking AMG-specific radiator grille with vertical struts in high-gloss chrome, which is a clear statement of the driving dynamics typical of the brand. At the same time, the pronounced "shark nose", the sharply cut new headlamps and the bonnet with powerdomes and AMG emblem set the dynamic tone.

Interior

Striking interior for performers.

The high-quality AMG interior intensifies the personal connection between driver and vehicle with its sporty appointments and MBUX infotainment system. Profiled AMG Performance seats emphasise the racing character and promote an active driving position with firm lateral support. The upholstery in the combination of ARTICO man-made leather/black MICROCUT microfibre is classic and sets hallmark AMG highlights
