Since the car was invented, we have put our innovational energy to the test over and again - with pioneering technologies, exemplary safety, top quality and reinvigorating comfort. It is only through our objective to bring something truly pioneering onto the road that a normal innovation becomes an innovation by Mercedes-Benz.

Mercedes-Benz vehicles are optimally designed for the wind: the design and silhouette ensure ideal aerodynamics - and have a positive effect on the range. That is why the all-electric EQE has proven to have extremely low air resistance in the wind tunnel. Mercedes-Benz door handles help here, too, and are integrated flush into the door when the vehicle sets off. The local emissions of our electric cars are the only thing lower: zero.
