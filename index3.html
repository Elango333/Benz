Welcome to the world of
Design.

We create desirable and aesthetic cars since the invention of the brand more than 120 years ago. Since the first day Mercedes-Benz is a luxury brand with aesthetic soul.

What people understand as luxury varies from person to person. It's about exquisite materials, quality, the best workmanship, traditional craftsmanship, the latest technologies and future-oriented design.

Design is essential for highly traditional luxury brands. Our get a luxury experience in the style of our premises - the Sensual Purity.

We permanently redefine ourselves to achieve the Extraordinary - we call it X- Factor. This is the only way we can preserve our unique style and to sparke desire.

"Our goal is, to build the most desirable cars in the world, for the brands of the Mercedes-Benz Group"

Mercedes-Benz Design sets new impulses in luxury and satisfies a desire for beauty and the extraordinary. This outlook is reflected most recently in the EQS, the Mercedes-AMG SL, the Mercedes-Maybach S-Class, the VISION EQXX, Project Geländewagen & Project MAYBACH and the Vision AMG.
